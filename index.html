What Kinyl does
Six things a $300k/yr
ops director would handle

Kinyl combines all seven of ARK Invest's "Big Ideas 2026" into one product built specifically for the 500 million small businesses traditional software has ignored.

Always-on observation
Reads your emails, invoices, calendars, and sales data continuously. You never have to tell it what to watch — it already knows.
Voice-first communication
Talks to vendors, clients, and staff by phone or message on your behalf. Manages full relationship cycles end to end.
Proactive interventions
Surfaces actions before problems happen — not after. Overdue invoices, price changes, scheduling risks, contract renewals.
Continuous business health
Monitors your business vitals the way a health tracker monitors your body. Cash flow, churn, margins, and team signals — always live.
Hyper-personalized to you
Learns your tone, your preferences, your risk tolerance. Adapts its style to match yours — formal, casual, cautious, or bold.
Messy data, no problem
Reads handwritten notes, scanned receipts, WhatsApp threads, and PDFs. Turns chaos into structured intelligence automatically.
